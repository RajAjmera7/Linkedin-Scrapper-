Quick Start Onboarding Guide

1

Configure Search Queries

Go to the "Search Queries" tab in the sidebar. We preloaded two active queries. You can create custom Boolean strings targeting your specific tech stack or skill sets.

2

Run the Scraper & Log In

Click "Run Scraper" in the top bar. A Chrome browser window will open on your desktop. Go to the "Scraper Console" tab in the app sidebar to watch the logs. Log in to your LinkedIn account in the Chrome window. Once you reach the feed, the scraper automatically takes over and closes Chrome when done!

3

Review Leads & Generate Pitches

Scraped posts appear on your Dashboard. For each lead, read the post content, click "View Post" to open it on LinkedIn, or click "Draft Pitch" to get a custom cold outreach message tailored to their request!

4

Update Status and Save Session

Classify your leads into "Saved", "Contacted", or "Ignored". Your login credentials and cookies are saved locally in the browser context folder, meaning future scraper sweeps will bypass login entirely!
